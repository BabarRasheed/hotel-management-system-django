About Greed's Hotel


Greed's Hotel in Argao, Cebu, Philippines, was founded by Celso M. Puerto Jr. and Maria Zyma Saphy Legaspi Quipao in 1995. Situated on a hill, the hotel offered stunning views of the Bohol Strait. Combining modern comfort with Filipino cultural elements, the hotel became popular for its hospitality and commitment to showcasing the local heritage.
